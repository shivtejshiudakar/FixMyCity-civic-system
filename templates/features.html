Platform Features

Built with modern civic technology to ensure seamless reporting and efficient resolution.

Location Tagging

Pinpoint the exact location of the issue using your device's GPS or by dropping a pin on our interactive map to ensure crews find exactly what needs fixing.

Image Evidence

A picture is worth a thousand words. Upload photos of the pothole, graffiti, or broken pipe directly from your mobile phone or desktop.

Smart Routing

No more bounced emails. Our backend categorizes your report and instantly notifies the correct departmental staff responsible for the fix.

Real-time Updates

Stay in the loop. Receive notifications when your issue changes status from Pending to In Progress, and finally to Resolved.

Data Analytics

Public dashboards with beautiful Chart.js visualizations show city wide resolution metrics, ensuring transparency and departmental accountability.

Mobile Responsive

Built to work flawlessly on any device. Report issues on-the-go with our mobile-optimized web interface without needing to download an app.
