Our Story

Born from Civic Necessity

FixMyCity was created with a simple premise: citizens are the best eyes and ears of a city. For too long, reporting a pothole, a broken streetlight, or illegal dumping meant navigating a maze of phone numbers, emails, and bureaucratic red tape.

We've transformed this outdated process into a streamlined, digital-first experience. Our platform allows anyone with a smartphone or computer to instantly report an issue, track its progress, and see the results—fostering transparency and accountability in local government.
